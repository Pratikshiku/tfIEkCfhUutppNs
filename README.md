## Nursing Home Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/26b7943f91c1457ebdd736fb24fb8447/ghb20250917) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Elderly, Family Member, Administrator.

**Elderly Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Personal Center
5. Change Password
6. Room Information
7. Safety Information
8. Dietary Information
9. Physical Examination Management
10. Medication Management
11. Needs Bag Information
12. Entry/Exit Information
13. Announcement Information

**Family Member Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Personal Center
5. Change Password
6. Elderly Management
7. Family Feedback
8. Room Information
9. Safety Information
10. Dietary Information
11. Physical Examination Information
12. Entry/Exit Information
13. Announcement Information

**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Administrator Management
3. User Management
4. Family Feedback Management
5. Room Information Management
6. Safety Information Management
7. Dietary Information Management
8. Physical Examination Management
9. Medication Management
10. Needs Bag Management
11. Entry/Exit Information Management
12. Announcement Information Management
13. Dictionary Table Management
